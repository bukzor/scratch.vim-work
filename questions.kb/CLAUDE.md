# Open Questions

Unresolved questions that will eventually become decisions (050) or get
dissolved by new facts (000). Each file states the question, what's known,
what's still uncertain, and what would resolve it.

## What Belongs

Things we're actively investigating or deferring. A question graduates to a
decision when the answer is clear. A question gets deleted when it becomes
moot.

## What Does NOT Belong

- Resolved choices (→ decisions.kb/050-technical-decisions.kb/)
- Idle curiosity (→ .claude/ideas.kb/)
