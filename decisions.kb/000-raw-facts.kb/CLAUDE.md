# Raw Facts

Empirical observations about the user's life and working environment. These are
true regardless of what we decide. Not up for debate — only for verification.

## What Belongs

Things that are the case: observed patterns, environmental constraints,
biological/cognitive realities.

## What Does NOT Belong

- Commitments or preferences (→ 005-constitutional-values.kb/)
- Conclusions drawn from facts (→ 010-design-principles.kb/)
