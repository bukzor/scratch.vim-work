# Constitutional Values

Commitments and priorities given the raw facts. These are choices about what
matters — they could in principle be different, but we've decided they aren't.

## What Belongs

Non-negotiable commitments: "given that X is true (000), we value Y."

## What Does NOT Belong

- Raw facts (→ 000-raw-facts.kb/)
- Derived principles (→ 010-design-principles.kb/)
- Concrete technical choices (→ 050-technical-decisions.kb/)
