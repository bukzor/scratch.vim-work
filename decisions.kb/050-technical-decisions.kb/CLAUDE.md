# Technical Decisions

Concrete choices that constrain implementation. Each records what was chosen,
why, and what it means going forward. Traceable to design principles in 010.

## What Belongs

A resolved choice: "we're doing X." Should reference the principles that drove
the choice.

## What Does NOT Belong

- Guiding principles without a concrete choice (→ 010-design-principles.kb/)
- Functional needs or requirements (→ needs.kb/)
- Evaluated options that haven't been chosen (→ needs.kb/{need}.kb/)
